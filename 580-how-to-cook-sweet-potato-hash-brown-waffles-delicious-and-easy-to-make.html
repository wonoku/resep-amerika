Ingredients of Sweet Potato Hash Brown Waffles

  1. Prepare 3-4 of medium size sweet potatoes.
  2. Prepare 1 of small yellow onion, skin removed and grated.
  3. Prepare 1 of egg, beaten.
  4. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of pepper.
  6. You need 1 tablespoon of olive oil or coconut oil, plus more for greasing waffle iron.
  7. Prepare of Preheat waffle iron on medium-high and grease very well.

Sweet Potato Hash Brown Waffles instructions

  1. Peel the sweet potatoes and shred them with a cheese grater, or even faster in a food processor..
  2. Peel and finely chop the onion..
  3. Mix potatoes and onion..
  4. Add 1 egg and mix well..
  5. Add salt and pepper..
  6. Spread 1/3 of mixture in waffle iron. Close, give a short but firm press, and cook until done, about 5 minutes depending on waffle iron.Remove from waffle iron, and repeat with remaining batter. Re-grease waffle iron between each waffle. Garnish with sour cream or maple syrup..
