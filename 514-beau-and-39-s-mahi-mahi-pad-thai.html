Beau's mahi mahi pad Thai step by step

  1. Dice one half of the onion and garlic.
  2. Olive oil in large saute pan or wok and brown onion and garlic.
  3. Add fish and cook evenly until it's cooked.
  4. Transfer cooked fish and onion to a clean bowl.
  5. Cut sliced other half of Onion and diced garlic.
  6. Olive oil in the same pan again and brown onion and garlic.
  7. Add a few drops of sesame oil And fish sauce.and about a table spoon of soy sauce.
  8. Add a tbspn of red chili sauce and enough pad Thai sauce that it's evenly covered in sauce.
  9. Add the cooked mahi and cooked rice noodles and stir well.
  10. Garnish with bean sprouts, crushed peanuts, and lime.
