Brad's cheese fried fish instructions

  1. cut fish into serving size pieces.
  2. mix panko, cheese, garlic, and salt..
  3. mix rice flour with water. depending on flour u might need a little more water. should be the consistency of pancake batter. season with a little salt..
  4. dredge fish in tempura, then roll in panko mix. lightly press crumbs into fish..
  5. fry in 360Â°F oil until golden brown..
  6. drain on paper towels. serve with dipping sauce of choice. i used tartar sauce and creamy french dressing..
