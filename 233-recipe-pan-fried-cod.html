Pan Fried Cod step by step

  1. Marinate fish fillets in the Italian dressing for 1 hour..
  2. Combine corn meal, old bay, onion powder, parsley, and lemon pepper in a medium bowl..
  3. Coat fillets in corn meal mixture..
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Avocado oil has a higher burn temperature and works better than olive oil for this..
  5. Place fillets in skillet and cook for approximately 15 minutes. Flip halfway through..
  6. Serve with a side of lemon wedges and enjoy!.
