Ingredients of Kedgeree- simple and quick

  1. Prepare 2 of fillets smoked haddock- in chunks.
  2. It's 1 of onion chopped.
  3. You need 2 of eggs.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of curry powder.
  5. You need of Frozen peas.
  6. It's 1 packet of pre-cooked rice.
  7. You need Handful of spinach.
  8. Prepare of Olive oil.
  9. You need 100 ml of milk.

Kedgeree- simple and quick instructions

  1. Boil a saucepan of water, once boiling add the eggs. After 6 minutes place them in a bowl of cold water..
  2. Drizzle some oil in a pan and gently fry the onions. After 3 mins add the curry powder and gently cook for 3 more minutes.
  3. Add the frozen peas, stir and cook until defrosted.
  4. Throw in the fish chunks and cook until the fish turns opaque and flakes.
  5. Crumble in the rice, pour in the milk, throw in handful of spinach and cook for 4 minutes until all combined. Squeeze in half a lemon and Season to taste..
  6. Meanwhile, peel the eggs. Gently tap them on a chopping board until the shell is cracked all over, then the shell should peel off easily.
  7. Spoon the kedgeree into bowls and slice the runny egg in half over the top!.
