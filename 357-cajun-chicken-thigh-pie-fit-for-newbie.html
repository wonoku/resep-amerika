Ingredients of Cajun Chicken Thigh Pie

  1. You need 2 of chicken thighs (diced).
  2. Prepare 1 of red pepper (diced).
  3. You need 2 of carrots (diced).
  4. Prepare 1 of jalapeño (diced).
  5. You need 1 of onion (diced).
  6. It's 3 of green onions.
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of Cajun seasoning.
  8. You need of Cornbread.
  9. Prepare 3/4 cup of corn muffin mix.
  10. It's 1/4 cup of cheese.
  11. It's of Green part of green onion.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of water.

Cajun Chicken Thigh Pie instructions

  1. Oil pan. Combine chicken and all vegetables (except for green part of green onions) into a pan on medium-high heat. Add seasoning. Cook and stir for 8 minutes. Add 2 teaspoons of corn mix at the end and stir. Then add some water and simmer for a 1-2 minutes..
  2. Transfer the mix to a baking pan. Evenly top the pan with the cornbread mix and bake for 20 minutes at 400. Let set and it’s done. Garnish with green onion..
