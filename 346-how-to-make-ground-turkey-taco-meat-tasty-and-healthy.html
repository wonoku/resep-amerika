Ingredients of Ground turkey taco meat

  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon of onion powder.
  5. You need 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  6. You need 1/2 teaspoon of ground pepper.
  7. You need 1 teaspoon of dried oregano.
  8. You need 1/2 teaspoon of paprika.
  9. It's 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil.
  11. Prepare 1 of small onion diced.
  12. It's 1 lb of ground turkey.
  13. It's 1 can of diced green chiles (4 oz).
  14. Prepare 1 can of beans rinsed and drained (black, pinto, or kidney).
  15. You need 1 cup of frozen corn.

Ground turkey taco meat instructions

  1. Stir together chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, oregano, paprika, and cayenne pepper together in a small bowl. Set aside..
  2. Heat olive oil in a LARGE skillet set over medium heat, add diced onion and stir. Add turkey next, break the meat up into crumbles as it cooks. Stir everything up as needed until no pink remains and the meat is fully cooked..
  3. Evenly sprinkle the taco seasoning mix over the turkey in the skillet. Raise the heat to medium-high..
  4. Next add cup of corn, diced green chiles, and beans. stir ensuring all ingredients are mixed together. (I usually use pinto but black beans and kidney work great as well.) NOTE: If ingredients appear to be very dry you can add a tablespoon or two of salsa, water, or broth to moisten..
  5. Let everything simmer for a few minutes. Turn off heat and serve..
