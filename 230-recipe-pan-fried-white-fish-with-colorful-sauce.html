Pan Fried White Fish with Colorful Sauce instructions

  1. Cut the red and yellow bell peppers and celery into 8 mm dice. Chop up the onion coarsely..
  2. Put the cut up vegetables from Step 1 and the ● ingredients into a bowl, and microwave for 2 minutes at 600 W..
  3. Season the fish with salt and pepper, and dust it lightly with flour..
  4. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, and pan fry the fish over low heat until browned on both sides..
  5. Transfer the fish to serving plates, pour plenty of the sauce from Step 2 over them, and serve..
