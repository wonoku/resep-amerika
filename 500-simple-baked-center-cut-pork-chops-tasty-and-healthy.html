Ingredients of Simple Baked Center Cut Pork Chops

  1. It's 4 of Center cut pork chops.

  3. Prepare 1 of Black pepper, to taste.
  4. Prepare 1 of Garlic powder, to taste.
  5. It's 1 of Fresh or dried Rosemary.
  6. It's 3 tbsp of Olive oil.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of Any white wine.

Simple Baked Center Cut Pork Chops step by step

  1. Sprinkle each pork chop, both sides, with salt, black pepper, garlic powder and Rosemary..
  2. Place pork chops in a baking dish or roasting pan and drizzle with olive oil..
  3. Cover with aluminum foil (to keep moist) and bake in a 350° oven for 45 mins. to an hour, or until internal temperature is 165°..
  4. Turn chops halfway through cooking..
  5. Add white wine the last 10 minutes, or so. But not too soon as it will dry out the meat..
