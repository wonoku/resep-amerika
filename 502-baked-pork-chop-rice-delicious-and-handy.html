  2. You need 2 each of onions.
  3. You need 10 of garlic.
  4. Prepare 50 grams of butter.
  5. Prepare 7 of pork chop.
  6. It's 2 large of bottle of Prego tomato spaghetti sauce. (make sure is enough to cover the rice).
  7. Prepare 3 cup of mushroom (Depends on how much mushrooms you want it).
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup of rosemary.
  9. Prepare 2 cup of Mozzarella cheese (depends on how much cheese you want it).
  10. Prepare 5 tbsp of soya sauce (optional).
  11. You need 1 dash of salt.

Baked pork chop rice step by step

  1. cook the rice in the rice cooker like you always do. Leave it aside to cool.
  2. Tomato sauce: chop one onion and 4 garlic. Fry them with olive oil. slice mushroom then add it into the pan to stir fry.. once the mushroom is half cooked, pour in Prego tomato spaghetti sauce.. bring it to simmer then it off and leave it aside.
  3. Pork chop: Marinate pork with soya sauce, salt, sugar and rosemary. leave it for at least 3 hours, best is overnight in fridge. Heat up the pan with oil, fry it till 80% cooked. Leave it aside.
  4. Fried Rice: In another pan, put in butter. Once hot, put in the rest of the chopped garlic and onion. stir fry till golden brown. Add in rice and fry till golden brown. add salt for a little seasoning. turn off heat and put it aside.
  5. Combine: Preheat oven till 200 Celsius. Line the baking tray with fried rice. Place the pork chop on top. Pour tomato sauce over. Spread Mozzarella cheese over and put it in the oven. Bake the rice till the cheese turn brown. Remove from heat and serve.
