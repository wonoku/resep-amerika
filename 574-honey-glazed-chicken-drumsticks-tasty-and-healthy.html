Honey glazed chicken drumsticks instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°f.
  2. Wash your chicken off drain and put the chicken in a large bowl ...add 2 tablespoons of oil and mix around.
  3. Add the chopped garlic ..black pepper.. salt.. onion powder.. 1 teaspoon of season salt.. 1teaspoon garlic powder and massage it in the chicken with your hands.
  4. In a small bowl add the egg yolk honey 1/2 teaspoon of season salt 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder and melted butter and whisk together.
  5. Save a 1/4 of the honey mixture in a cup for later.
  6. In a deep baking dish lightly grease it with some butter and line your chicken up in it.
  7. Brush on the honey mixture making sure to get both sides of the chicken.
  8. Bake uncovered @ 325 for 40 minutes.
  9. When the 40 minutes is up take your chicken out and up your temperature to 375° f... turn the chicken over and brush on the remaining honey mixture and put it back in the oven for 35 more minutes and enjoy !.
