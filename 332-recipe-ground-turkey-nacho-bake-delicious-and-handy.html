Ground turkey nacho bake step by step

  1. Cook your already thawed ground turkey. When browned; add 3/4 c of water and taco seasoning to cooked turkey..
  2. Pre-heat oven for 350°F.
  3. While your ground turkey is cooking; cut up your vegetables..
  4. Heat up vegetarian refried beans in microwave for 1 to 2 minutes. Set aside to cool..
  5. Get out baking pan. Put corn tortilla chips on pan. Add 1/2 ground turkey on chips; refried beans; 1/2 veggies; add black olives; add 1/2c of sour cream (save the other half for next layer); spread with knife and then sprinkle cheese on top..
  6. Add second layer of tortilla chips. Repeat step 5; while adding the second half of sour cream; beans; and veggies. Add cheese on top. Bake for 10 minutes..
