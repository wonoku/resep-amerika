The garlic cooks quickly in 20 seconds (maybe less because it depends on how popular the pan is). If the garlic is cooked too long, then it will taste not very pleasant and reduce its odor. If you wish to add garlic, make sure to add it last, so it will not burn.

Ingredients of Chili Sweet Potato Fries

  1. Prepare 4 of large sweet potatoes.
  2. It's 1 (14 oz) of can of corn.
  3. Prepare 1 (14 oz) of can black beans drained and rinsed.
  4. You need 1 (15 oz) of can diced tomatoes.
  5. You need 1 lb of ground beef.
  6. Prepare 1 of yellow onion diced.
  7. Prepare 2 cloves of garlic minced.
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp of chili powder.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of paprika.
  10. Prepare 2 tsp of garlic powder.
  11. You need 2 tsp of black pepper.
  12. Prepare 1 cup of shredded cheddar.
  13. Prepare 1 cup of sour cream.
  14. It's 2 tbsp of olive oil.

Chili Sweet Potato Fries step by step

  1. Cut sweet potatoes into smallish french fries, put into a large bowl and toss with pepper, garlic powder, and oil. Put on a cookie sheet in one layer and bake at 450° for 20-25 min..
  2. Cook beef in a large skillet until done and drain. Add corn, beans, diced onion, diced tomatoes, garlic, paprika, and chili powder. Simmer for 20 min..
  3. Salt fries, top with chili, add sour cream and cheese and serve..
