Ingredients of Cow boy burger on French loaf

  1. It's 1 lb of ground beef.
  2. It's 1 of French onion soup packet.
  3. Prepare 4 slices of cheese (I used Swiss but cheddar is probably the best choice).
  4. Prepare 1 of French bread loaf.
  5. It's 2 cups of bbq rib meat.
  6. You need of Bbq sauce for drizzling.
  7. It's of Onion rings (see my recipe).

Cow boy burger on French loaf instructions

  1. Mix French onion soup mix with burger meat and shape into 4 patties. Cook until no longer pink..
  2. Cut the French bread into 4 pieces and slice them open to make a “bun”.
  3. Assemble it: bread, patty, cheese, onion rings, bbq ribs, more bbq sauce and top with remaining half of bread..
