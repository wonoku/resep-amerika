Ingredients of Avocado, Strawberry, Banana Cocktail

  1. It's of Avocado.
  2. Prepare 2 of Avocados, ripe.
  3. You need 2 of Bananas, ripe.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of Honey.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of Milk.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of Cold Water.
  7. It's of Strawberry.
  8. It's 250 grams of Strawberry.
  9. It's 1 of Banana.
  10. It's 1 tbsp of Honey.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of Cold Water.
  12. Prepare of Garnish.
  13. You need 1 of Pine Nuts, Almonds, Strawberry.

Avocado, Strawberry, Banana Cocktail instructions

  1. In a food processor blend avocado, banana, honey, milk, and water till smooth. Spoon in cups..
  2. In food processor blend strawberry, banana, honey and water till smooth. Spoon on top of avocado..
  3. Garnish with pine nuts, almonds, and strawberry pieces. Serve cold..
