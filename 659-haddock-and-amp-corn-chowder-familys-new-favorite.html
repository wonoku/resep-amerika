Ingredients of Haddock & Corn Chowder

  1. It's 2 pieces of smoked haddock.
  2. It's 1 can of or 2 ears corn.
  3. Prepare 1 of small potato diced.
  4. It's 1 of small white onion diced.
  5. You need 1 tsp of minced garlic.
  6. It's 250 ml of cream.
  7. You need 1 of chilli chowed.
  8. It's of Small handful coriander.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of olive oil.
  10. Prepare 4 tbsp of chopped celery.
  11. Prepare 4 of spring onions.
  12. You need of Salt and pepper to tase.

Haddock & Corn Chowder instructions

  1. Get your ingredients ready!.
  2. Heat oil to medium heat and add onions and soften.
  3. Add garlic, celery and chilli to the soften onions. Cook for about 3 mins.
  4. Add corn and potatoes to the pot and cook with a little water for about 10 mins.
  5. Add the cream and cook till the potatoes start to cook away and thicken the cream.
  6. Poach the haddock in the cream, add some water if it is too thick. Should be a watery consistency..
  7. Garnish with spring onions and coriander, enjoy.
