Ingredients of Japanese-style Loco Moco with Mahi Mahi

  1. You need 400 g of Mahi mahi.
  2. Prepare 3 Tbsp of Potato starch.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of Lemon juice.
  4. Prepare 1 pinch of Salt and pepper.
  5. It's 1 clove of Garlic, grated.
  6. It's 2 of servings cooked rice.
  7. You need 1 Tbsp of Soy sauce, plus more to taste.
  8. It's 1 tsp of Sugar.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of Mirin.
  10. It's 2 tsp of Mayonnaise.
  11. You need 1/4 sheet of Nori seaweed.
  12. It's 2 leaves of Cabbage or lettuce.

Japanese-style Loco Moco with Mahi Mahi instructions

  1. Remove any skin from mahi mahi and cut into smaller pieces..
  2. Put mahi mahi into a plastic baggy and add the lemon juice, a little salt & pepper, and garlic. Mix well. Just before frying, add the potato starch into the bag and shake the contents to coat the fish..
  3. Heat oil in a saucepan and fry the mahi mahi until crispy on both sides and cooked through..
  4. Mix the soy sauce, sugar and mirin. Add rice to a bowl and layer finely strips of cabbage or lettuce and mahi mahi over top. Dress with mayo, thinly cut strips of nori and the soy sauce mixture..
