Ingredients of Easy Grilled Fish Packets in Foil

  1. Prepare 4 of Mahi Mahi (or other white fish) fillets, about 5 oz. each.
  2. It's 4 tsp of olive oil.
  3. You need to taste of Salt and fresh pepper,.
  4. It's 4 sprigs of fresh herbs, parsley, rosemary, oregano.
  5. It's 1 of lemon, sliced thin.
  6. You need 4 of large pieces heavy duty aluminum foil, about 18 to 20 inches long.
