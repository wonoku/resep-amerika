Ingredients of low calorie teriyaki chicken drumsticks

  1. Prepare 8 of skinned chicken drumsticks.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of reduced sodium soy sauce.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of Brown sugar.
  4. It's 1 clove of garlic, chopped.
  5. It's 1 tsp of ginger.
  6. You need 1 1/2 tsp of cornstarch.
  7. You need 1 of salt and pepper to taste.

Make an easy teriyaki sauce for these chicken drumsticks with soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic and ginger.

low calorie teriyaki chicken drumsticks step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. combine soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and cornstarch in sauce pan. add 2 tablespoons of water.
  3. whisk over medium heat until thickened. about 2 minutes.
  4. coat casserole dish lightly with nonstick cooking spray.
  5. add chicken to dish and top with the prepared sauce.
  6. bake about 30 minutes or until juices run clear. I like to turn the broiler on for 5-10 minutes at the end to really caramelize the sauce..
