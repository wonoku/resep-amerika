Brad's potato parmesan crusted cod step by step

  2. Double dip cod portions. First egg then crumbs, repeat. Place on a greased foil lined baking sheet.
  3. Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes. Remove and plate. Sprinkle with lemon juice and salt to taste..
