Ingredients of Crockpot Sweet Potato üç† Black Bean Chilli

  1. It's 4-5 pounds of sweet potatoes (about 5 big sweet potatoes).
  2. You need 1/8 teaspoon of black pepper.
  3. You need 2 tablespoons of chilli powder.
  4. It's 1 tablespoon of ground cumin.
  5. It's 1 can (28 ounce) of crushed tomatoes.
  6. It's 1 can (16 ounce) of refried beans.
  7. Prepare 2 cans (15.5 ounce) of black beans, undrained.
  8. You need 1 can (14.5 ounce) of chicken broth (low sodium).
  9. You need 2 of onions, sliced.
  10. It's 1 of green pepper, chopped.
  11. Prepare 6 cloves of garlic, minced.
  12. It's of Shredded cheddar cheese.
  13. It's of Sour cream.

Crockpot Sweet Potato üç† Black Bean Chilli instructions

  1. Rinse and peel your sweet potatoes, then cut into chunks.....
  2. Place sweet potato chunks into large crockpot........
  3. Add to sweet potatoes your black pepper, chilli powder and ground cumin......
  4. Now add your crushed tomatoes, refried beans, black beans and chicken broth......
  5. Now add your sliced onions, green pepper and minced garlic, stir well.....
  6. Cover and cook on Low heat for 8 hours, stirring occasionally......
  7. Serve topped with shredded cheddar cheese and sour cream and enjoy üòâ!!!.
