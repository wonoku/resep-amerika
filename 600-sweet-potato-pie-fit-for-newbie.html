Ingredients of Sweet Potato Pie

  1. It's 1 cup of mashed sweet potatoes.
  2. You need 1 stick of butter.
  3. It's 1.5 cups of sugar.
  4. It's 3 tablespoons of flour.
  5. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  6. It's 1 of small can evaporated milk.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of lemon extract.
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon of vanilla.
  9. It's 1 of unbaked pie crust.
