20 mins: ground turkey marinara sauce pasta step by step

  1. Cook the pasta following the instruction on the package..
  2. (While waiting for water to boil/cooking the pasta) add 1.5 tbsp olive oil on a big saucepan over medium high heat. Add garlic and onion and cook for 2 minutes..
  3. Add olive oil and ground turkey and all the rest of the ingredients to the sauce pan. Stir fry until the turkey is fully cooked (start to brown/golden brown), usually 6 to 8 minutes..
  4. Turn the heat down to low and add the bottle of sauce. Continue to stir for a minute or two..
  5. Mix with the cooked pasta and dinner (lunch) is served :D.
