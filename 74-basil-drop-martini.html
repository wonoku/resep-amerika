Ingredients of Basil drop martini

  1. Prepare 4 of basil leaves torn.
  2. Prepare 2 oz of vodka.
  3. Prepare 1 oz of lime juice.
  4. Prepare .05 oz of simple syrup.
  5. It's of Ice.
  6. You need of Garnish.
  7. Prepare 2 of basil leaves.

Basil drop martini instructions

  1. In a shaker with ice add the torn basil, vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup..
  2. Cover and shake vigorously..
  3. In a tumbler glass with ice empty the shaker with the straining lid on..
  4. Top with basil leaves..
  5. Drink up and enjoy!!.
