Ingredients of Chipotle ranch chicken fajitas

  1. It's 1 pound of chicken tenderloins.
  2. It's 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 1 of large onion sliced.
  4. You need 2 of red peppers sliced.
  5. You need 2 tablespoons of powdered ranch dressing mix.
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon of chipotle garlic seasoning.

Chipotle ranch chicken fajitas step by step

  1. Combine oil and spices and pour over chicken in a large ziplock bag. Seal bag, mix together and marinate overnight.
  2. Remove chicken and place on baking sheet. Put sliced onion and peppers in the bag and shake until coated.
  3. Put pepper and onions on same baking sheet and put in the oven 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
  4. Eat with or without tortilla shells.
