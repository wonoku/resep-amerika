Crumb Topped Grilled Cod Fillets step by step

  1. Preheat grill to medium.
  2. Make Crumb Mixture.
  3. Combine all crumb ingredients in a bowl and,ix with a forl.
  4. Male Coating Sauce.
  5. Combine all sauce ingredients in a small bowl and mix together.
  6. Prepare fish.
  7. Coat fish on all sides lightly with sauce.
  8. Press crumbs only on top of fish.
  9. Grill bottom of fish on grill without flipping, covering grill until fish is just cooked through and has moist juicy flakes. The time will depend on the thickness of the fish.
  10. Serve with melted butter and lemon slices.
