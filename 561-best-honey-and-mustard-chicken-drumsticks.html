Ingredients of Honey and mustard chicken drumsticks

  1. Prepare 6 of chicken drumsticks.
  2. You need 1 of Salt and pepper to season.
  3. You need 2 tbsp of oil.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of honey.
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of dijon mustard.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of White wine or rice vinegar.
  7. It's 1 tsp of sugar.
  8. You need 50 ml of chicken stock.
  9. You need 3 of garlic cloves crushed.
  10. You need 1 of chopped fresh parsley or dry mixed herbs.

Honey and mustard chicken drumsticks instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180C or 350°F..
  2. Place chicken drumsticks in an ovenproof dish..
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper on each drumstick and brush oil evenly on each one..
  4. In a small bowl mix the honey, mustard, white wine/rice vinegar, chicken stock, sugar and garlic and mix..
  5. Pour the mix onto the chicken. Make sure the chickens are evenly coated..
  6. Sprinkle the herbs evenly on each chicken..
  7. Put the chicken into the oven for 35-40 minutes. Flip the chicken over half way into the cooking..
  8. Take the chicken out of the oven and it is ready to serve. I had this with quinoa salad, which worked together nicely..
