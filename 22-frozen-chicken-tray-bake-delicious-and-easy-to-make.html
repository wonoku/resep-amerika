Ingredients of Frozen chicken tray bake

  1. You need 1 tbsp of kosher salt.
  2. It's 1/2 tbsp of ground black pepper.
  3. You need 1 tbsp of Italian seasoning.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of hot paprika.
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of sage.
  6. Prepare 4 of chicken leg quarters, bone-in and skin-on.
  7. It's of Extra virgin olive oil.
  8. You need 2 of medium carrots, peeled and cut into 1/4 in. slices.
  9. You need 1 cup of frozen peas.

Frozen chicken tray bake step by step

  1. Combine the salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, paprika and sage in a small bowl. Pull the frozen chicken pieces apart and rub them down with olive oil, then sprinkle on the seasoning mix until each quarter is well covered..
  2. Put the chicken in a baking tray and cover with foil, then put it in an oven set to 350 F (no need to preheat). Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes..
  3. Take off the foil and continue baking the chicken uncovered for 30 minutes..
  4. Remove the chicken to a plate. Add the carrots and peas to the tray and toss them in the drippings. Scrape up any chicken bits stuck to the tray. Spread the veg into a single layer and lay the chicken quarters on top. Put it back in the oven for a final 30 minutes, then serve..
