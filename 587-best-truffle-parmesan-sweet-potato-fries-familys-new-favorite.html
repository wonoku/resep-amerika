Ingredients of Truffle Parmesan Sweet Potato Fries

  1. Prepare 3 of sweet potatoes cut into fries (Japanese sweet potatoes work best but normal are fine).
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  3. It's 2 tablespoons of truffle oil.
  4. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  5. It's to taste of Fresh ground pepper.
  6. You need 1 tablespoon of grated Parmesan cheese.

Truffle Parmesan Sweet Potato Fries step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F and line a baking sheet with tin foil.
  2. Toss potatoes with olive oil salt and pepper. DO NOT add truffle oil at this time.
  3. Place potatoes on tray and bake until golden brown and crispy (about 20-30 min).
  4. Place potatoes in large bowl and drizzle truffle oil, grated cheese and additional salt and pepper (if needed).
  5. Note: balsamic glaze and garlic aioli serve as great dipping sauces. Ketchup is not ideal :).
