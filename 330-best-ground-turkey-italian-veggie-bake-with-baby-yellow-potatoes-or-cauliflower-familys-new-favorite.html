Ingredients of Ground Turkey Italian Veggie Bake with Baby Yellow Potatoes Or Cauliflower

  1. It's 1 lb of ground turkey or beef.
  2. It's 1 of organic green beans.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of diced green peppers.
  4. It's 1 cup of organic diced tomatoes.
  5. It's 3/4 cup of sliced black olives.
  6. Prepare 495 grams of baby yellow potatoes.
  7. You need 1/2 cup of diced mushrooms.
  8. You need 6 tbsp of tomato paste.
  9. You need 2 tbsp of reduced fat cream cheese.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of low fat mozzarella cheese.
  11. You need 1 of salt and pepper.
  12. It's 1 of rosemary.
  13. It's 1 of italian seasoning.
  14. Prepare 1 of oregano.
  15. It's 1 of basil.
  16. It's 1 tbsp of olive oil.

Ground Turkey Italian Veggie Bake with Baby Yellow Potatoes Or Cauliflower instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325.
  2. Cook ground turkey in olive oil..
  3. In a small pot heat green beans and baby potatoes or cauliflower to soften. (Organic green beans can be stiff).
  4. When ground turkey is done add diced tomatoes and tomato paste mixing with turkey..
  5. Add green peppers, olives, mushrooms, seasonings except salt and pepper..
  6. Add cream cheese and 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese to the ground turkey mixture..
  7. Drain green beans and cut potatoes/cauliflower into smaller chunks. Place in oiled baking dish..
  8. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Add turkey/veggie mixture on top of green beans..
  9. Add remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese. Bake for 30 minutes..
