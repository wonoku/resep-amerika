Garlic butter baked pork chop instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Salt and pepper both sides of your pork chops.
  3. Get a fry pan screaming hot with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. fry/brown both sides of pork chops approximately 2 minutes each side..
  4. In another pan mix, and heat your butter, garlic, and thyme..
  5. After pork chops are browned, cover chops with garlic butter sauce and put chops in oven for approximately 12 minutes, check Internal tempura to be 145..
